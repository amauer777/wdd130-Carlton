Content

Super Smash Bros' Lucas reigns from Nintendo's Earthbound series. Childlike in appearance he packs a punch in Smash Ultimate, becoming a viable fighter with his strong move set. His move set may appear similar to another character Ness, however he features strengths Lucas lacks. Here you can find a guide on Lucas' Basic move set, and his more Advanced combos to dominate in Super Smash Bros Ultimate.

Home

Information that will be used, completely unorganized. People will come to this website to get simple information on how to play Lucas from Super Smash Bros Ultimate. Lucas is a advantageous character featured in Super Smash Bros Ultimate. Coming from the game Earthbound or also known as the Mother series. He is a blond child with childlike attacks. Using magical fire, electricity, and star-power. He is similar to another character Ness also from the Earthbound games. However Lucas' kit features many pros in comparison to Ness. With lighter attacks that carry opponents and allow for more stage control and dominance over opponents. He has Basic combos that can be learned quickly and set a foundation for playing Lucas. His more advanced combos are difficult to master and have weaknesses, however are incredibly strong and flashy when executed correctly. As well some moves are referred to as Meteor Crash moves. These moves plunge opponents downward, making it incredibly difficult to recover and return to stage.

Basic Moveset Guide

triple tap combo: - A + A + A - A 3 tap combo - x damage sprint attack: - -> + -> + A - Quick sprinting attack dealing - x damage up-tilt: - ^ + A - A kick above Lucas' head with a small hit box - x damage

Advanced Moveset Guide
