For players looking to advance Lucas' move set. These moves expand creativity and are much stronger in confirming kills. They are more flashy and impressive when executed correctly. However they can at times come with high risk and open weaknesses when done incorrectly.

  • Triple kick combo
  • In air + D-pad down + A - 3 kicks below Lucas, carries opponents between kicks
  • x damage
  • Back flip kick (meteor)
  • In air + <- + A - 3 kicks above, to the side, and below Lucas, that carries opponents between kicks
  • x damage
  • Sends players downward z-air whip
  • In air + -> or <- + Z - an in air grab, that mashes Lucas into players cluttering another player's movement
  • x damage triple
